Studies

  • 09/2011-07/2013 Life Cycle Analysis(LCA) of building carbon emission

    In this research, an audit tool was developed based on BIM (Revit) to estimate buildings' life-cycle carbon emission in design stage. Most of my work was on design scenarios of building's life cycle and finding suitable formulas as carbon emission data to calculate buildings' carbon emission.

  • 09/2010-06/2011 BIM on Industrialized apartments (IA)

    Our group established a design system for industrialized apartments (IA) in which every component of the building is standardized. I developed a Revit plug-in through C# to accomplish the IA design. This tool could help architects to optimize the materials cost of the building through an inventory.

  • 10/2011-08/2012 research on the green rating systems(GRS) for residential buildings in China.

    I was involved partially in drawing up the first draft of GRS for residential buildings in China. Majority of my work is the analysis of currently mainstreaming GRS and analyzed the downside and upside of them to supply evidence for the new GRS in China.
